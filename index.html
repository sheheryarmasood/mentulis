What makes us different

Support that meets you where you are.

Whoever you are, hard days come in your own language. And if you're far from home — homesickness at 2am, interviews in your third language, missing weddings and funerals back home — that load is real, and most wellness apps were never built with you in mind. Mentulis is.

  • An AI companion that understands what you're going through — in your language, not just translated
  • Evidence-based CBT exercises, self-checks, and gentle daily habits
  • Speak instead of typing — and have replies read aloud in your language
How it works

Feeling better, three simple steps in.

1

Choose your language

Pick from nine languages on first launch. Everything — chat, tools and voice — speaks the language you think and feel in.

2

Check in & talk it out

Log your mood in one tap, or open the AI companion any hour to put what's heavy into words and feel heard.

3

Build gentle habits

Breathing, journaling and CBT exercises help, a little each day — and you can share a clean summary with a therapist when you're ready.

What's inside

Small tools. Real comfort.

Everything in Mentulis is designed to take five minutes or less — because everyday life is busy enough.

AI Companion

A warm, judgement-free listener available any hour — and it understands the things that weigh on you, from everyday stress to homesickness and starting over.

Voice in your language

Talk instead of typing, and hear replies aloud — in English, French, Punjabi, Hindi, Mandarin, Tagalog, Arabic, Spanish or Urdu.

Mood tracking

One tap a day. Watch your week take shape, notice patterns, and bring real insight to your next appointment.

CBT toolkit

Thought records, worry journal, gratitude log and clinically-used self-checks (GAD-7, PHQ-9) — therapy techniques, self-serve.

Guided breathing

Box breathing, 4-7-8 and calm patterns with a soothing animated guide — for racing minds and sleepless nights.

Crisis support

One tap to Canadian crisis lines — 9-8-8, Kids Help Phone, Hope for Wellness and 2-1-1 — always free, always there.

You are not alone
Belonging

Millions have made this journey. None of them did it alone.

Mentulis isn't a replacement for friends, family or professional care — it's the quiet support in between. A place to put feelings into words at 3am, prepare for hard conversations, or simply hear "that makes sense" in your mother tongue.

When you're ready for more support, the app helps you find therapists who speak your language, and turns your mood history into a clean PDF you can hand to any professional.

Privacy first

Your journal never leaves your phone.

Moods, journal entries and exercises are stored only on your device. No ads, no data sales, no analytics trackers. Optional backup goes to your own Google Drive — not to us.
